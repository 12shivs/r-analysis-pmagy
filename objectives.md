RESEARCH OBJECTIVES
1. To evaluate the socio-economic changes in the selected 'Adarsh Gram' villages of Madhya Pradesh, since the implementation of PMAGY.
2. To assess the effectiveness of infrastructure development initiatives under PMAGY in targeted villages.
3. To analyze the impact of PMAGY on education and health facilities in the selected villages.
4. To examine the role of PMAGY in enhancing the livelihood opportunities for the residents of the selected villages.
5. To investigate the challenges faced in the implementation of PMAGY in these villages.
6. To suggest recommendations for improving the implementation and outcomes of PMAGY based on the findings from the selected villages.
